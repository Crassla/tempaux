How I Work

Based on my many years practical business experience, I deliver meaningful, hands-on support for running your business.

My services are best suited to working with you over a long term period to assist you to succeed in your marketplace. Specific services include:

Pay monthly: Contracts offering you up to two days a month at your office or other place of business, providing business planning, advisory and other management consultancy services targeted towards your specific objectives.

On demand: Consultancy and specific project delivery services available on a fixed fee basis, offering a flexible and cost effective way to fill specific gaps in your business.

Business Training: Specific, one-off fee for the delivery of training sessions for you and your team. All training sessions are tailored to your specific business and deliver practical tools and guidance participants can use immediately in their "day jobs".
